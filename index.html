About Josh

For more than two decades, Josh Eckhaus has served as a mentor, advocate, strategist, diplomat, and amateur therapist to families navigating the college admissions process. He's worked with families around the world, building a full-spectrum admissions program and helping applicants find their perfect fit at a wide range of colleges and universities.

Josh genuinely loves the individuality, authenticity, and creativity inherent in the college application process. As a writer, a former actor, an academic, and a lifelong musician, he is comfortable working with everyone, from painters and poets to scientists and soccer players.
